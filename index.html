Author Bio

Ross Evans writes epic stories where myth and reality collide. Once an IT engineer focused on AV and video conferencing, he traveled widely and eventually settled in Tennessee, still chasing the quiet moments where worlds feel thin.

Trading Existence began as a single idea and became a gateway: a series about creation, choice, and the people who make us want to become the best version of ourselves.
