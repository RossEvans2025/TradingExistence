The Core Idea

The Original World

Every realm—magical or technological—is a fractured reflection of one place: The Original World. When the veil between realms weakens, reflections collide—myth, memory, magic, and machine.

These collisions are not accidents. They are symptoms of a system beginning to fail.

The Architect

The First (Chron)

The First is the original God of creation—the architect of the Original World. Once a creator, now an executioner.

“Creation was a mistake. I will correct it.”

The Veil Between Worlds

The barrier that separates realities—and the reason everything is unraveling.

The Fracture

When Worlds Touch

The Veil was designed to keep reflections isolated. As it tears, worlds bleed into one another—dragons into cities, soldiers into myths, gods into men.

Consequences

Unstable Reality

Magic behaves unpredictably. Technology fails in unfamiliar ways. History itself becomes unreliable as memories overlap across realms.
